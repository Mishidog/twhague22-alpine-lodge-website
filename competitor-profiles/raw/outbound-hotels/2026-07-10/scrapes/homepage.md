# Outbound Hotels Homepage - Research Notes

Source: https://outboundhotels.com/
Reviewed: 2026-07-10

## Observed Positioning

- Collection of lifestyle hotels in outdoor destinations.
- Promises reconnection through good times, people, outdoor adventure, and local culture.
- Uses the recurring brand line "Outside is just the beginning."
- Locations include Jackson Hole, Mammoth Lakes, Stowe, Yosemite, Sedona, and Park City.
- Extends the brand beyond rooms through retreats, destination editorial content, awards, and social media.

## Observed Visual System

- Full-bleed cinematic destination video in the opening viewport.
- Oversized white wordmark over landscape footage.
- Black, white, forest green, and bright orange palette.
- Condensed uppercase navigation and CTA typography paired with a characterful serif for editorial headings.
- Wide landscape photography dominates; buildings appear as gateways into the setting.
- Property imagery focuses on gathering spaces, firepits, pools, cabins, and social atmosphere.

## Conversion Structure

- Persistent Book Now control.
- Destination selector separates brand discovery from property booking.
- Location modules provide distinct Book and Visit paths.
- Press recognition and awards provide third-party validation.
- Editorial destination content and social feeds extend discovery beyond the reservation funnel.
